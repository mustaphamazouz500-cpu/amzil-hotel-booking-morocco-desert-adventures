🙋 Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our tours and services

How do I book a tour? ▼
Use our Booking page or Contact us directly via WhatsApp. We'll confirm within 24 hours.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept cash (Euro/MAD), bank transfer, PayPal, and credit cards. A small deposit may be required for advance bookings.
Is hotel pickup included? ▼
Yes! All our tours include round-trip transport from your hotel in Marrakech (for Agafay, Imlil). Merzouga tours include local pickup.
What should I wear in the desert? ▼
Light, breathable clothing during the day. Warm layers for evening (desert nights can be cold). Comfortable shoes, sunglasses, and sunscreen recommended.
Can children join the tours? ▼
Absolutely! Most activities are family-friendly. Quad biking has a minimum age of 12 for solo driving, but children can ride with an adult.
What languages do your guides speak? ▼
Our guides are multilingual — English, French, Arabic, and Tamazight (Berber) as standard. Some also speak Spanish and German.
Can I customize my itinerary? ▼
Yes! We specialize in custom tours. Contact us with your preferences and we'll create a personalized itinerary.
