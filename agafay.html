🏜️ Agafay Desert

Just 40 minutes from Marrakech lies a stunning rocky desert — your gateway to quad biking, camel rides, and magical dinners under the stars

Why Visit Agafay?

Unlike the sandy Sahara, Agafay offers a unique lunar-like landscape of rocky dunes and golden hills. It's the perfect desert escape without the long drive — ideal for half-day or evening adventures. Experience Berber hospitality, thrilling quad bike rides, serene camel treks, and unforgettable sunsets over the Atlas Mountains.
