⛰️ Imlil & Atlas Mountains

Discover the High Atlas — hike through Berber villages, conquer Mount Toubkal, and breathe the purest mountain air in North Africa

Why Visit Imlil?

🏔️

Gateway to Mount Toubkal

Imlil is the starting point for climbing Mount Toubkal (4,167m) — North Africa's highest peak. Even if you don't summit, the valley offers spectacular hiking trails for all levels, from gentle walks through apple orchards to challenging mountain treks.

🏘️

Authentic Berber Culture

Visit traditional Amazigh (Berber) villages nestled on mountain slopes. Experience genuine hospitality, learn about centuries-old farming methods, taste homemade bread with olive oil, and sip mint tea while gazing at panoramic mountain views.

🌿

Nature at Its Purest

In spring and summer, the valleys burst with wildflowers, walnut groves, and cherry orchards. Crystal-clear streams flow from snow-capped peaks. In winter, the mountains dress in white — a surreal contrast to the desert just hours away.
